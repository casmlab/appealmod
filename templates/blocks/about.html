About Us

AppealMod was designed by researchers at the University of Michigan School of Information in collaboration with Reddit moderators. AppealMod is the result of an elaborate design process involving multiple moderators of different subreddits, as well as a real-world evaluation in r/pics over four months. Please read our research paper to learn more about AppealMod's design process.

This research project is funded by the National Science Foundation.
The expansion of AppealMod on Reddit is supported by the Center for Social Media Responsibility.
